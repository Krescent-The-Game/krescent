Moonshot endless runner

Originally conceived for GitHub Game Off 2020 — drive a moon buggy across an alien surface, dodge rocks and moondust, shoot enemies, and chase your best score. Reimagined for the AI era.

Play locally Controls & docs

Gameplay

Endless moon run

Procedural obstacles — rocks, floating dust, enemies. Health drops to zero and your best score is saved.

WebGL powered

Built with BabylonJS 4 and vue-babylonjs — scene-tree 3D rendering in the browser. Phase 2 ports to Babylon 7 + Vite for Vercel play-in-browser.

By m3yevn & kaichie
